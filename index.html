Common Questions

Frequently
Asked

Still have questions? Reach out and we'll answer them in our consultation call.

Ask Us Directly
What types of projects do you handle?
+
We design modern tropical homes, fine dining restaurants, mixed-use commercial buildings, townhouse developments, cultural facilities, and more. We serve both residential and commercial clients across the Philippines.
How much does it cost to hire an architect?
+
Fees vary by project scope, size, and services required. We offer a free initial consultation where we can discuss your project and provide a transparent fee proposal. We tailor our approach to fit your goals and budget.
How long does the design process take?
+
A typical residential project takes 2–4 months for design development and documentation. Commercial projects may take longer. We'll give you a detailed timeline during your consultation based on your specific project.
Do you handle building permits?
+
Yes. We prepare all signed and sealed documentation required for building permits in the Philippines, and we coordinate with local government units on your behalf to keep the process moving smoothly.
Do you work with clients outside Metro Manila?
+
Absolutely. We work with clients across the Philippines, including Batangas, Pampanga, Bataan, and beyond. Many of our most meaningful projects have been outside Metro Manila.
Can you help if I already have a contractor?
+
Yes. We can work alongside your existing contractor, providing design and documentation services, site visits, and construction support to protect your investment and ensure the design is built as intended.
